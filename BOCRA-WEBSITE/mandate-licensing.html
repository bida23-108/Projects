Mandate

Licensing

Licence categories, applications and verification.

Licensing Framework

BOCRA is mandated by Section 6(h) of the CRA Act to process applications for, and issue, licences, permits, permissions, concessions and authorities for regulated sectors: telecommunications, internet, radio communications, broadcasting and postal.

Network Facilities Provider (NFP)

Licensees own, operate or provide physical infrastructure — fixed links, satellites, submarine cable, fibre/copper, towers, switches, base stations — for own use or other licensed operators.

Services & Applications Provider (SAP)

Non-infrastructure providers offering speech, data, text, image and application services to end users over NFP infrastructure. Excludes broadcasting content services.

Content Services Provider (CSP)

Provides content material — audio, text, images, video — solely for broadcasting (TV and radio) and other information services, including Subscription TV.

Postal Licensing

Two categories: Designated Postal Operator (DPO) with universal service obligations, and Commercial Postal Operator (CPO) for value-added courier and postal services.
