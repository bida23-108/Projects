01 — Executive Summary

A Quarter of Accelerated Innovation

The first quarter of 2026 recorded significant regulatory activity across all three licensed mobile network operators in Botswana. BOCRA processed fifteen product and service applications within the quarter, spanning mobile data revamps, digital content platforms, 5G fixed broadband upgrades, financial services integration, and emergency short code allocations.

Key Trend
Operators prioritised data value over price increases — every revamp delivered more volume at the same or lower cost
Notable First
BTC's Travel eSIM marks Botswana's first locally-issued international data-only eSIM service for travellers
Digital Inclusion
BTC's SMEGA platform advances financial inclusion by enabling USSD-based wallets on any handset — no smartphone required
Community Focus
Mascom's Temo service represents a deliberate move into agri-tech, serving Botswana's farming community via USSD

"All fifteen applications were reviewed and approved within the quarter, reflecting BOCRA's commitment to timely regulatory processing and enabling operators to deliver improved services to Botswana consumers."

All My Friends 7 & 30-Day Promotion
A 100% data bonus promotion on Orange's popular social media bundles. Customers received double the daily data cap — from 500 MB to 1 GB per day — at no additional cost, via USSD or MaxIt app.

Botswana Telecommunications Corporation
4 Services Approved

BTC made notable strides in innovation during Q1 2026, introducing the country's first Travel eSIM product, two emergency and healthcare short codes, and a comprehensive financial inclusion platform. All four applications were approved on or before 31 March 2026.

Innovation  ·  Industry First
BTC Travel eSIM Service
Botswana's first locally-issued international data-only eSIM service, available through a dedicated mobile application (Android & iOS). Customers — including Botswana residents and international travellers — can purchase, activate and manage international data bundles digitally, without a physical SIM card, at predefined per-destination rates denominated in USD.

The first quarter of 2026 demonstrated a dynamic and evolving telecommunications landscape in Botswana.
